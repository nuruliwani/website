MY EXPERIENCE AT UITM

I WANNA TELL ABOUT MY EXPERIENCE OF UNIVERSITY LIFE

LUCKILY, THE APPLICATION PROCESS FOR THIS COURSE WAS STRAIGHT-FORWARD. THIS COURSE DID NOT REQUIRED ANY INTERVIEWS OR TEST. I AM VERY HAPPY, BECAUSE I USED TO DREAM TO CONTINUED MY STUDIES IN THIS COURSE.

EVERYONE'S FIRST SEMESTER AT UNIVERSITY IS DIFFERENT. MY EXPERIENCE HAS BEEN A MIX OF BOTH POSITIVE AND CHALLENGING TIME. THE TRANCISION FROM SCHOOL TO UNIVERSITY WAS A BIT DIFFICULT. GENERALLY, I'VE ENJOYABLE MOMENT. HOWEVER, LIKE MOST STUDENT, I HAD FIRST TIME NERVES AND MISSED HOME FAR MORE THAN I EXPECTED.

ON THE FIRST SEMESTER, I AM REALLY LUCKY, BECAUSE ONE OF MY ROOMMATE IS ACTUALLY SAME COURSE WITH ME. WE BECOME A GOOD FRIEND. WE ALWAYS STUDY AND DO ASSIGNMENT TOGETHER. WE SPENDING MOST OF TIME IN UNIVERSITY TOGETHER. EVEN THOUGH NOW WE ARE NOT A ROOMMATE, WE ARE STILL GOOD FRIEND UNTIL NOW.

OUTSIDE MY STUDIES, I GET TO SEE MY FAMILY A LOT AND I AM ALSO STILL IN TOUCH WITH FRIEND FROM SCHOOL

That's all about my experience at Uitm.
